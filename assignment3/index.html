iPad - Procreate App

This piece is a completely original piece created during a daily drawing challenge. The boy in the painting is peering into a soft drink bottle, to find a miniature astronaut within their own outer-space environment. This piece explores the wonder and awe of the mundane, and how beauty and adventure can appear in unexpected places.
